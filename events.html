Events in Sønderborg

Sønderborg has cultural, historical and family-friendly events during the whole year. Below you can see some of the main events and a simple calendar for December.

Upcoming Events

Christmas Market

Date: 5 December

Place: Sønderborg Town Square

A traditional Christmas market with handmade crafts, warm drinks, food stalls, music, and a festive atmosphere.

Castle Christmas Exhibition

Date: 12–13 December

Place: Sønderborg Castle

Enjoy a holiday-themed exhibition inside the historic castle, with decorations, storytelling and guided tours.

Winter Light Walk

Date: 19 December

Place: Harbour Promenade

A beautiful evening walk along the harbour with lights, music, and cosy Christmas feeling.

New Year Celebration

Date: 31 December

Place: Sønderborg Harbour

Join the New Year's Eve celebration with fireworks and live music by the water.
