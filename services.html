Transportation

Sønderborg offers bus connections, bike rental stations, ferry routes and nearby airports. Public transport is accessible and well connected.

Healthcare Services

The city provides easy access to emergency services, pharmacies, and medical centers. Most pharmacies offer extended opening hours.

  • Emergency hospital
  • Local clinics
  • Dental services
  • 24/7 pharmacies

Tourist Information

The tourist information center can help with maps, tickets, guided tours and general questions about the city. It is located near the harbour.

  • City maps and brochures
  • Help with sightseeing plans
