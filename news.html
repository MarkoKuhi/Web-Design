News in Sønderborg

Here are the latest news and updates from Sønderborg. Newest stories appear at the top.

Sønderborg Prepares for New Year Celebrations

10 December 2025

The city is getting ready for the annual New Year celebration at the harbour. Workers are installing lights, food stands and a special stage for live music on New Year's Eve. A large number of visitors is expected this year.

Winter Light Walk Brings Hundreds to the Harbour

5 December 2025

The Winter Light Walk was a big success as hundreds of people enjoyed illuminated installations by the waterfront. Families, tourists and students joined the event to experience music, warm drinks and a cosy Christmas feeling.

Castle Christmas Exhibition Opens at Sønderborg Castle

3 December 2025

The annual Christmas exhibition at Sønderborg Castle has opened its doors. Visitors can walk through decorated rooms, see historical Christmas items and learn about Danish holiday traditions. Guided tours are available.

Christmas Market Attracts Visitors to the Town Square

1 December 2025

The Christmas Market in Sønderborg started this week with stalls selling crafts, sweets and warm drinks. Many families visited during the opening day, attracted by the lights, music and holiday atmosphere.
