NEWS IN SØNDERBORG

Sønderborg Prepares for New Year Celebrations

10 December 2025

The city is getting ready for the annual New Year celebration at the harbour. Workers are installing lights, food stands and a special stage for live music. A large number of visitors is expected this year.

Winter Light Walk Brings Hundreds to the Harbour

5 December 2025

The Winter Light Walk was a big success as hundreds of people enjoyed illuminated installations by the waterfront. Families, tourists and students joined the event to experience music, warm drinks and a cosy Christmas feeling.

Castle Christmas Exhibition Opens at Sønderborg Castle

3 December 2025

The annual Christmas exhibition at Sønderborg Castle has opened its doors. Visitors can walk through decorated rooms, see historical Christmas items and learn about Danish holiday traditions. Guided tours are available.

Christmas Market Attracts Visitors to the Town Square

1 December 2025

The Christmas Market in Sønderborg started this week with stalls selling crafts, sweets and warm drinks. Many families visited during the opening day, attracted by the lights, music and holiday atmosphere.

SDU Expands in Sønderborg

The University of Southern Denmark (SDU) is opening a new building next to the Alsik Hotel. The new facility will support engineering programs and innovation labs, strengthening the city's role as a technology hub.

Sønderborg Opens New Sauna

Sønderborg’s popular waterfront has gained a new attraction this week as the city officially opened its upgraded public sauna. Located just a few steps from the fjord, the facility aims to promote well-being, outdoor culture, and the growing interest in winter bathing among both residents and visitors.
